## Key Terms

### Payment Terms
- **Invoice Payment**: 45 days after Google receives correct invoice
- **Invoice Deadline**: Must submit within 180 days after Inspection Period ends
- **Inspection Period**: 10 business days after service delivery
- **Taxes**: All payments are inclusive of applicable taxes

### Liability Limits
- **Aggregate Cap**: Greater of US$1,000,000 or 12 months' fees
- **Excluded**: Indirect, consequential, punitive damages

### Termination
- **For Breach**: 30-day cure period for material breach
- **For Convenience**: Google may terminate with written notice
- **Effect**: Google pays for services invoiced before termination date

### IP Rights
- **Developed IP**: Owned by Google
- **Deliverables**: Title transfers to Google upon delivery
- **Background IP**: Each party retains ownership
