# US Stock Broker & Crypto Funding Analysis for HK Investors

## Overview

Comprehensive analysis for Hong Kong citizens looking to invest in US stocks/ETFs with funding from crypto (cold wallet) and HKD cash. Focus on long-term investment (5-year hold, <10 trades/year).

## Investor Profile

| Factor | Status |
|--------|--------|
| Citizenship | Hong Kong |
| Existing Accounts | Futu, Kraken, Citibank Plus, Hang Seng, BOC |
| Funding Source | Crypto (cold wallet) + HKD cash |
| Investment Style | Long-term (5 years), <10 trades/year |
| NOT interested in | IPO, short-term trading |

---

## Part 1: Broker Selection

### Key Decision Factors

| Factor | Why It Matters |
|--------|----------------|
| **Trading Fee** | Low-frequency trading = per-trade minimum matters more than % |
| **Forex Rate** | Converting HKD → USD can cost 0.3%+ at bad rates |
| **Fee Stability** | 5-year hold needs predictable costs, not promos |
| **Platform Reliability** | Long-term = need stable, established platform |
| **Ecosystem Diversification** | Don't put all assets with one provider |

### Broker Comparison

| Broker | Commission | Platform Fee | Min/Trade | Forex Spread | Promo Risk |
|--------|------------|--------------|-----------|--------------|------------|
| **IBKR** | $0.0035/share | None | **$0.35** | **~0%** | None |
| Tiger | Free | Free | $0 | ~0.2% | Ends Dec 2025 |
| Futu | $0.0049/share | $0.005/share | $1.99 | ~0.3% | None |
| Longbridge | Free | $0.005/share | $1.00 | ~0.2% | None |

### Annual Cost Comparison (10 trades/year, $50K portfolio)

| Broker | Trading Cost | Forex Cost (if converting $50K) | Total |
|--------|--------------|--------------------------------|-------|
| **IBKR** | ~$3.50 | ~$0-5 | **~$5-10** |
| Tiger | $0 (2025) / Unknown (2026+) | ~$100 | ~$100+ |
| Futu | ~$20 | ~$150 | ~$170 |
| Longbridge | ~$10 | ~$100 | ~$110 |

### ✅ Decision: IBKR

**Reasons:**
1. **Best forex rate** — saves $100-150/year vs competitors on currency conversion
2. **Lowest stable trading fee** — $0.35 min vs $1-2 at others
3. **No promotional uncertainty** — Tiger's free trading ends Dec 2025
4. **Ecosystem diversification** — different from existing Futu account
5. **Professional platform** — suitable for long-term, low-frequency investing

## Part 2: Crypto Platform Selection

### Key Decision Factors

| Factor | Why It Matters |
|--------|----------------|
| **Existing Account** | Avoid new KYC/setup time |
| **Trading Fee** | One-time cost for conversion |
| **Withdrawal Options** | Wire to HK bank feasibility |
| **Liquidity** | Large conversion needs good depth |

### Platform Comparison

| Platform | Trading Fee | Accept Cold Wallet | Wire to HK | Status |
|----------|-------------|-------------------|------------|--------|
| **Kraken** | ~0.2% | ✅ Yes | ✅ USD Wire | **Have account** |
| Victory Securities | 0.22% | ✅ Yes | ✅ FPS | Need new account |
| Hashkey | 0.237% | ✅ Yes | ✅ FPS | Need new account |

### Cost Comparison ($50K USD)

| Platform | Trading Fee | Transfer Fee | Total | Time |
|----------|-------------|--------------|-------|------|
| **Kraken** | ~$100 | ~$35 wire | **~$135** | 3-7 days |
| Victory | ~$110 | $0 FPS | ~$110 | 1-2 days |
| Hashkey | ~$118 | $0 FPS | ~$118 | 1-2 days |

### ✅ Decision: Kraken

**Reasons:**
1. **Existing account** — no new KYC, familiar platform
2. **Competitive cost** — only ~$20-25 more than HK alternatives
3. **Proven reliability** — established exchange with good liquidity
4. **USD wire capability** — can send directly to Citibank USD account

## Part 3: Funding Transfer Method

### Key Decision Factors

| Factor | Why It Matters |
|--------|----------------|
| **Existing Bank Accounts** | Leverage what you have |
| **Wire Fees** | Incoming international wire costs vary by bank tier |
| **FPS Flexibility** | Free instant transfers between HK banks |
| **Simplicity** | Fewer steps = less friction |

### Bank Comparison

| Bank | USD Account | Incoming Wire Fee | FPS Limit | Status |
|------|-------------|-------------------|-----------|--------|
| **Citibank Plus** | ✅ Yes | ~$100-200 HKD | $100K/day | **Have account** |
| Hang Seng | ✅ Yes | Varies | $400K/day | Have account |
| BOC | ✅ Yes | Varies | $400K/day | Have account |

### Transfer Path Options

| Path | Steps | Total Fee | Time |
|------|-------|-----------|------|
| **Kraken → Citibank USD → FPS → IBKR** | 3 | ~$35 + wire fee | 3-7 days |
| Kraken → Direct to IBKR | 2 | ~$35 | 3-7 days |
| Kraken → Hang Seng → FPS → IBKR | 3 | ~$35 + wire fee | 3-7 days |

### ✅ Decision: Kraken → Citibank Plus USD

**Reasons:**
1. **Existing USD account** — Citibank Plus has USD account ready
2. **Flexibility** — can hold USD or FPS to other banks/brokers
3. **Visibility** — see funds in familiar banking app before moving to broker
4. **Backup option** — if IBKR has issues, USD is still accessible in Citibank

## ✅ Action Checklist

### Setup (One-time)
- [ ] Open IBKR HK account at [interactivebrokers.com.hk](https://www.interactivebrokers.com.hk)
- [ ] Complete IBKR verification (ID, address proof)
- [ ] Verify Citibank Plus USD account is active
- [ ] Note Citibank USD account wire details (SWIFT, account number)
- [ ] Verify Kraken withdrawal settings and limits

### Crypto Funding Execution
- [ ] Transfer crypto from cold wallet → Kraken
- [ ] Sell crypto → USD on Kraken
- [ ] Initiate USD wire: Kraken → Citibank Plus USD account
- [ ] Wait 3-7 business days for wire arrival
- [ ] Confirm USD received in Citibank Plus

### Move to Broker
- [ ] FPS transfer from Citibank → IBKR (if HKD)
- [ ] Or wire USD directly from Citibank → IBKR
- [ ] Confirm funds in IBKR account

### Start Investing
- [ ] Convert any HKD → USD in IBKR (use limit order for best rate)
- [ ] Research and select US stocks/ETFs
- [ ] Execute purchases
- [ ] Set calendar reminder for annual portfolio review
