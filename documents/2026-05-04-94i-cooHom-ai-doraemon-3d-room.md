## 📝 Notes & Key Takeaways

### Main Insights

- **CooHom AI** (酷家樂) is a Chinese AI-powered interior design platform that can auto-generate a 3D room model from a 2D floor plan image — including manga/anime floor plans
- Full workflow: **2D floor plan scan/image → import to CooHom → AI generates 3D model → manual refinement → AI render → Gemini style variations**
- Period accuracy matters: the creator adjusted floor height to match Showa-era Japanese housing proportions (Doraemon is set in ~1970s Japan)
- Multi-level alignment is a known pain point — the video shows a specific technique to fix upper/lower floor misalignment
- Gemini AI is used at the *end* of the pipeline for creative expansion (different photorealistic style outputs from the same 3D base)
- The result is strikingly photorealistic — the anime room becomes a fully detailed interior design render
